Process

How LifeLink Works

Four simple steps connect donors to those in critical need — in minutes, not hours.

01

Donor Registers on Platform

Blood donors sign up with their name, contact, blood group, city, and availability status. Your details are securely stored and only shared during genuine emergencies. Toggle availability on/off anytime — no pressure, no obligation.

⏱ Takes 2 minutes
02

Patient Posts an Emergency Request

A patient, family member, or hospital staff posts a blood request with the required blood group, hospital name, city, number of units needed, and urgency level. The request goes live instantly and is visible to all matching donors.

🚨 Live in 30 seconds
03

Nearby Donors Get Alerted

Donors matching the blood group and city automatically receive an email alert. They can view the full request details, confirm availability, and connect directly with the hospital — no middleman, no delay.

📧 Email alerts via EmailJS
04

Donation & Life Saved

The willing donor visits the hospital and donates. The request is marked as fulfilled. The donor's last donation date is updated automatically, respecting the 90-day recovery period. One act of kindness — three lives saved.

💚 3 lives per donation

Differentiators

Why LifeLink is Different

⚡
Real-Time Matching

Unlike static directories, LifeLink matches donors to requests in real-time based on blood group and city, with instant alerts.

🔒
Privacy First

Donor contact details are never publicly visible. Connections happen only during verified emergencies through our platform.

🏥
Hospital Integration Ready

Our architecture is designed to integrate directly with hospital blood bank systems for automated urgent requests.

🕐
Availability Management

Donors control their availability with a toggle. The system auto-marks them as resting after a donation.
